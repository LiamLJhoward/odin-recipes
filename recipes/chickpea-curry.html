For the slow cook:

  • 800g Chickpeas
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2tbsp Mild curry powder
  • 1 tsp Sweet smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp Mild chilli flakes
  • 3 tbsp Tomato Pur√©e
  • 400g Coconut milk
  • 1/2 tsp Sea salt

To finish:

  • 240g Bayb spinach
  • 50g Mango chutney
  • 1 tsp Garam masala

Instructions:

  1. Add the slow cooker ingredients to the pan and cook on high for 3 hours OR low for 6 hours.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients just before serving (the heat will wilt the spinach).
